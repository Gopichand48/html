How to Prepare Maggi

  1. Step 1: Boil 1½ cups of water in a pan.
  2. Step 2: Add Maggi noodles (without breaking them).
  3. Step 3: Add the tastemaker (flavoring) that comes with the pack.
  4. Step 4: Stir well and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Let it simmer for 1–2 more minutes until the water is mostly absorbed.
  6. Step 6: Turn off the heat and serve hot!
